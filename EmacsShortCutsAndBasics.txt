The Leader keys - Alt, Ctrl and Esc.
Ctrl+h  -help
Ctrl+g  -quit
Ctrl+x k -kill buffer/kill file
Ctrl+x Ctrl+f  -open file
Ctrl+x b (file name)  -to create new file/buffer
esc+number ctrl+{n,p}  -leidzia n eiluciu zemyn aukstyn
---To move, we use: arrows, Ctrl+n, Ctrl+p, Ctrl+f, Ctrl+b
---To select, we hold shift and press: arrows, Ctrl+n, Ctrl+p, Ctrl+f, Ctrl+b
---To Copy(kill and save) = Alt +w
---To Cut(kill) = Ctrl +w
---To Paste(Yank) = Ctrl +y
